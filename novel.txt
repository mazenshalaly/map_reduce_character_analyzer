Harry Potter was a young wizard who lived with his aunt and uncle. 
Harry had a best friend named Ron Weasley. Ron had red hair and came from a large family.
Hermione Granger was another close friend of Harry and Ron. Hermione was very smart.

Harry, Ron, and Hermione went on many adventures together. Harry faced many dangers.
Ron was always loyal to Harry. Hermione helped Harry solve difficult problems.

The three friends, Harry, Ron, and Hermione, studied magic at Hogwarts.
Harry became famous for defeating the dark lord. Ron supported Harry throughout.
Hermione's intelligence saved Harry and Ron many times.

Together, Harry, Ron, and Hermione proved that friendship and courage matter most.
